## Journal Details

### 1. Computers & Geosciences (RECOMMENDED)
- **Publisher:** Elsevier
- **Impact Factor:** 4.2
- **Scope:** Computational methods in geosciences, AI/ML applications
- **Why suitable:** Perfect match for deep learning + geophysics
- **Submission:** https://www.editorialmanager.com/cageo/
- **Format:** Max 8,000 words, figures separate

### 2. Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence
- **Publisher:** Elsevier
- **Impact Factor:** 8.0
- **Scope:** AI applications in engineering domains
- **Why suitable:** Strong AI focus, accepts geoscience applications
- **Submission:** https://www.editorialmanager.com/eaai/
- **Format:** No strict word limit, comprehensive studies welcome

### 3. Natural Hazards
- **Publisher:** Springer
- **Impact Factor:** 3.7
- **Scope:** Natural hazard assessment and mitigation
- **Why suitable:** Earthquake focus, accepts ML methods
- **Submission:** https://www.springer.com/journal/11069
- **Format:** Max 10,000 words

### 4. IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing
- **Publisher:** IEEE
- **Impact Factor:** 8.2
- **Scope:** Remote sensing, geoscience signal processing
- **Why suitable:** High prestige, accepts geomagnetic studies
- **Submission:** https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/tgrs
- **Format:** Max 30 pages double-spaced

## Submission Strategy

### Option A: High Impact First
1. Submit to **Engineering Applications of AI** (IF 8.0)
2. If rejected → **Computers & Geosciences** (IF 4.2)
3. If rejected → **Natural Hazards** (IF 3.7)

### Option B: Best Fit First
1. Submit to **Computers & Geosciences** (IF 4.2)
2. If rejected → **Natural Hazards** (IF 3.7)
3. If rejected → **Journal of Seismology** (IF 2.1)

### Option C: Fast Publication
1. Submit to **Scientific Reports** (IF 4.6, OA)
2. Typical review: 4-8 weeks
3. APC: $2,490
