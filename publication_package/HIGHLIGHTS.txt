HIGHLIGHTS
==========

• VGG16 achieves 98.68% accuracy for earthquake magnitude classification from geomagnetic spectrograms

• EfficientNet-B0 offers 26× smaller model size (20 MB vs 528 MB) with 94.37% accuracy

• LOEO and LOSO cross-validation confirm excellent generalization (97.53% and 97.57% respectively)

• Grad-CAM analysis confirms models focus on physically meaningful ULF frequency patterns (0.001-0.01 Hz)

• Multi-task learning enables simultaneous magnitude and azimuth prediction from single input

• Comprehensive failure analysis demonstrates physically interpretable model behavior
